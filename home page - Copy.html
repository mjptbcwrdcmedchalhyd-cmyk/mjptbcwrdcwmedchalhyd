Visionary Leadership – Our Principal

Principal’s Message

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Mahatma Jyothiba Phule Telangana Backward Classes Welfare Residential Degree College for Women, Medchal & Hyderabad. Our institution is committed to empowering young women through quality education, discipline, and strong moral values. We strive to create an environment that encourages academic excellence, leadership, innovation, and social responsibility.

At our college, we believe that education is not just about earning a degree, but about building character, confidence, and competence. With the support of our dedicated faculty and staff, we ensure that every student receives the guidance and opportunities needed to achieve her goals.

We are continuously working towards providing the best academic standards, modern facilities, and holistic development programs that prepare our students to face global challenges with confidence.

I warmly invite students and parents to be a part of our institution and join us in the journey towards knowledge, growth, and success.

Smt. Dr. Radha Velchuri
Principal

Why Choose MJPTBCWRDC?

At Mahatma Jyothiba Phule Telangana Backward Classes Welfare Residential Degree College for Women, we are committed to empowering young women through high-quality education, holistic development, and strong values. Here’s why students choose us:

  1. Academic Excellence: We offer a wide range of undergraduate programs with experienced faculty, modern teaching methods, and well-equipped labs. Our focus is not only on academic success but also on nurturing critical thinking and creativity.
  2. Dedicated Faculty & Mentorship: Our faculty members are highly qualified and committed to guiding each student individually. Personalized mentorship ensures that every student achieves her full potential.
  3. Holistic Development: Education at MJPTBCWRDC goes beyond textbooks. We encourage participation in cultural events, sports, community service, and leadership activities to develop confident and responsible women.
  4. State-of-the-Art Facilities: Our campus provides a safe and inspiring environment with well-maintained classrooms, library resources, laboratories, computer centers, and residential facilities for students.
  5. Women’s Safety & Security: We prioritize the safety and well-being of our students. The campus is equipped with security personnel, CCTV surveillance, controlled access points, and safe hostel accommodations, ensuring a secure and comfortable learning environment.
  6. Career & Placement Support: We prepare our students for the future through career guidance, skill-building workshops, and placement assistance. Our alumni have excelled in various professional fields and continue to inspire current students.
  7. Inclusive & Supportive Environment: We take pride in fostering an inclusive, friendly, and motivating atmosphere where students from all backgrounds feel welcomed and encouraged.
