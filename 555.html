Mahatma Jyothiba Phule Telangana Backward Classes Welfare Residential Degree College for Women, Medchal & Hyderabad, has a proud legacy of empowering young women through education, discipline, and values. Over the years, the institution has grown into a hub of academic excellence and holistic development.

Learn to Lead

At MJPTBCWRDC Medchal Hyderabad College, the motto Learn to Lead is more than a slogan—it is a philosophy that guides our academic and personal growth. Education here is envisioned as a holistic journey, nurturing character, confidence, and competence alongside knowledge.

Our leadership provides unwavering guidance, motivating us to cultivate diverse skills and inspiring us to advance in every sphere of life. With curiosity encouraged, innovation celebrated, and responsibility instilled, students are empowered to grow with resilience and confidence.

Ultimately, Learn to Lead becomes a lived reality, preparing us to contribute meaningfully to society and the future ahead.
