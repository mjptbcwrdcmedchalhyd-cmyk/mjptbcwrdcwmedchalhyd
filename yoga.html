YOGA

Yoga is an ancient practice that originated in India thousands of years ago. It is a combination of physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), and meditation that helps improve both physical and mental health. Yoga helps increase flexibility, strength, balance, and concentration, while also reducing stress and promoting relaxation. Many people practice yoga daily to maintain a healthy lifestyle and to achieve peace of mind. It is suitable for people of all ages and is widely practiced around the world as a way to improve overall well-being.
