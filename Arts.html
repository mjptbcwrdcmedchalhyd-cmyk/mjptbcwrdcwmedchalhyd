Department of History

About the Department

The Department of History focuses on the study of past civilizations, cultures, political movements, and global historical developments. The program prepares students for careers in education, research, civil services, archaeology, and heritage management.

With experienced faculty and a research-oriented curriculum, students gain deep knowledge in ancient, medieval, and modern history, historical research methods, archival studies, and critical analysis of historical sources.

Programs Offered

  • B.A (HEP)
  • B.A (PPGEP)

Department of Public Policy

About the Department

The Department of Public Policy focuses on policy analysis, governance, public administration, and social development. The program prepares students for careers in government, public service, NGOs, research institutions, and international organizations.

With experienced faculty and a policy-driven curriculum, students gain expertise in public administration, policy evaluation, economic and social policy design, data-driven decision making, and sustainable development strategies.

Department of Economics

About the Department

The Department of Economics focuses on economic theory, policy analysis, financial systems, and quantitative research. The program prepares students for careers in government, business, finance, research, and academia.

With experienced faculty, modern laboratories, and an industry-oriented curriculum, students gain practical exposure in statistical analysis, econometrics, policy evaluation, and global economic trends.

Department of Political Science

About the Department

The Department of Political Science focuses on political theory, governance, public administration, international relations, and comparative politics. The program prepares students for careers in civil services, law, public administration, diplomacy, journalism, and research.

With experienced faculty and a dynamic curriculum, students develop strong analytical skills in political analysis, constitutional studies, public policy, and global political systems.
