Department of English

About the Department

The Department of English is dedicated to academic excellence in the study of English language and literature. The department aims to develop students' communication skills, critical thinking, and creative expression.

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs that focus on literature, linguistics, literary theory, and language studies. Our faculty members are experienced educators and researchers committed to student success.

Department of Telugu

About the Department

The Department of Telugu is committed to promoting the rich heritage, literature, and cultural traditions of the Telugu language. The department aims to develop students' language proficiency, literary appreciation, and research skills.

Through academic programs and cultural activities, the department encourages students to explore classical and modern Telugu literature while strengthening communication and analytical abilities.
