Eligibility

  • Residence: Candidates must be residents of Telangana State.
  • Academic Qualification: Must have passed the Intermediate Examination (or equivalent) in the first attempt.
  • Marks Requirement: A minimum of 50% marks in aggregate and 40% marks in English are required. A 5% relaxation in marks is applicable for SC/ST students.
  • Income Limit: Parental income must not exceed ₹1,50,000 per annum for rural areas and ₹2,00,000 for urban areas.
  • Age Limit: Generally based on SSC completion, with preference for those graduating from junior college in the immediate academic year.
  • Categories: Eligible categories include BC, SC, ST, EBC, MBC, and Orphans.

Admission Process

  1. Fill the online application form
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Pay the application fee
  4. Attend counseling / interview
  5. Confirm admission
