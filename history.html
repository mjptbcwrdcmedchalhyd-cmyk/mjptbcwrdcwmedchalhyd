About Us

Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Telangana Backward Classes Welfare Residential Degree College (MJPTBCWRDC) was established in 2022 with the aim of providing quality higher education to students, especially from Backward Classes and underprivileged communities in Telangana.

Branches

  • Hyderabad
  • Medchal

Vision

  • To empower students through quality education.
  • To promote academic excellence and holistic development.
  • To create equal educational opportunities for all.

Mission

  • Provide residential and degree education facilities.
  • Encourage leadership, discipline, and social responsibility.
  • Support students with modern infrastructure and academic resources.

Facilities

  • Well-equipped classrooms
  • Digital learning resources
  • Library and sufficient labs
  • Residential hostel facilities
  • Experienced and dedicated faculty
