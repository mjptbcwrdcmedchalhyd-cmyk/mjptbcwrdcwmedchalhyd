B. Saidulu Garu(IFS)
Secretary Of (MJPTBCWREIS) T.S

    About

  • B. Saidulu is the Secretary of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Telangana BC Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society(MJPTBCWREIS), appointed in March 2024 to oversee BC Residential Schools
    • .
  • He belongs to 2005 batch Indian Forest service (IFS ) Officer

    • Appointment

  • Appointed on March 8th 2024, by the govt. of Telangana replacing Mallaiah Battu garu.

    • Resposibilities

  • His key responsibilities include overseeing the operations of BC Welfare Gurukulam(Residential) Schools, focusing on educational and boarding facilities for backward classes students in Telangana.
  • He has been involved in initiatives for educational collaborations to improve quality education in BC Residential Schools including projects targeting improved proficiency in STEM and language subjects.

    • Role

  • He is recognized as a leader of overseeing educational services for students in Telangana..
