Department of Commerce

About the Department

The Department of Commerce is committed to providing quality education in the field of business, finance, and accounting. It focuses on developing a strong foundation in core areas such as financial accounting, corporate accounting, taxation, business law, economics, and business management. The department aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to meet the challenges of the modern business world. Through a blend of theoretical learning and practical exposure, students gain a clear understanding of financial systems, market trends, and organizational practices.

Special emphasis is given to skill development through seminars, workshops, projects, internships, and industry interaction. The department encourages analytical thinking, problem-solving ability, and professional ethics among students. Graduates of the Commerce program are well-prepared to pursue careers in banking, finance, accounting, corporate sectors, entrepreneurship, and higher studies such as M.Com, MBA, CA, and other professional courses.

Programs Offered

  • B.Com (CA) – Bachelor of Commerce with Computer Applications
  • B.Com (BA) – Bachelor of Commerce with Business Analytics
