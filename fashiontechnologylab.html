About the Lab

  • The Fashion Technology laboratory provides a practical learning environment for developing skills in garment design and apparel production.
  • The facility includes sewing machines, dress forms, pattern drafting tools, and other equipment required for garment construction and design activities.
  • Students gain hands-on experience in body measurement, pattern development, garment assembly, and fashion illustration.
  • The lab helps students understand the complete process of garment creation from design to final product.

Importance of the Lab

  • Provides practical exposure to garment construction and apparel design.
  • Helps students apply theoretical concepts in real design situations.
  • Encourages creativity and innovation in textile and fashion design.
  • Supports skill development required for careers in the fashion and textile industry.
  • Enhances both artistic and technical abilities.

Lab Goals and Objectives

  • Provide a fully equipped environment for fashion design and garment construction.
  • Develop practical skills in body measurements and pattern drafting.
  • Encourage creativity through fashion illustration and textile decoration.
  • Support student learning in apparel production techniques.
  • Improve technical and aesthetic skills through hands-on practice.

Benefits of Using the Lab

  • Students gain real experience in garment designing and stitching.
  • Develop skills in pattern making, cutting, and garment assembly.
  • Learn fabric surface decoration techniques such as screen printing, block printing, and fabric painting.
  • Work with decorative materials like beads, sequins, and other embellishments.
  • Improve creativity, craftsmanship, and professional design skills.

Facilities

  • The lab includes sewing machines, dress forms, and pattern drafting tools for garment construction.
  • Workstations are provided for cutting, stitching, and designing garments.
  • Special areas are available for fabric decoration techniques and design practice.
  • The laboratory provides drawing tools such as pencils, sketch pens, and embroidery frames.

Equipment and Tools

  • Sewing machines for garment stitching and construction.
  • Dress forms for garment fitting and design development.
  • Pattern drafting tools for creating garment patterns.
  • Embroidery frames for embroidery practice.
  • Drawing tools such as pencils and sketch pens for fashion illustration.

Textile Decoration Techniques

  • Screen printing for fabric design.
  • Block printing for decorative textile patterns.
  • Dyeing techniques to create fabric colors and effects.
  • Fabric painting for creative textile artwork.
  • Use of beads, sequins, and embellishments to enhance garments.

Workspace

  • Organized workstations for garment cutting and stitching.
  • Comfortable seating for long practical sessions.
  • Separate areas for design, stitching, and textile decoration activities.
  • Instructor supervision for guidance and safety.

Fashion Technology Lab Rules

  1. Authorized Access Only: Only students and faculty are allowed to use the laboratory.
  2. Use Equipment Carefully: Sewing machines and tools must be handled carefully.
  3. Maintain Cleanliness: Keep the workspace clean and organized.
  4. Follow Safety Guidelines: All activities must follow established safety procedures.
  5. Handle Materials Properly: Use fabrics, dyes, and decorative materials responsibly.
  6. Return Tools After Use: All tools and equipment must be returned to their proper place.
  7. No Food or Drinks: Eating or drinking inside the lab is not allowed.
  8. Follow Instructor Instructions: Always follow guidance from the lab instructor.
