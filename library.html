About Library

The college library plays an important role in supporting students’ academic growth by providing a quiet place for reading, studying, and research. It contains a wide range of resources such as textbooks, reference books, journals, magazines, and digital materials for different subjects. Students use the library to gain knowledge, prepare for exams, and improve their reading habits. The peaceful environment helps them concentrate and learn independently. Overall, the college library encourages curiosity, supports learning, and helps students achieve better academic performance. There are total 5782+ books are there in our college.
