Zoology Lab

About the Lab

Our Zoology Laboratory, part of the Department of Zoology, provides students with both theoretical knowledge and practical experiences. Regular classes are conducted to help students understand zoological concepts deeply, and we specially focus on PG entrance preparation, guiding students towards their academic and career goals.

Importance of the Lab

  • Understand the practical applications of zoological concepts.
  • Develop hands-on skills for biological research.
  • Prepare effectively for postgraduate entrance examinations.
  • Gain exposure to modern zoological techniques and equipment.

Lab Goals and Objectives

  • Provide practical training in zoology experiments.
  • Enhance observational and analytical skills of students.
  • Encourage scientific thinking and research orientation.
  • Prepare students for higher studies and competitive exams.

Benefits of the Lab

  • Hands-on experience with biological specimens and experiments.
  • Improved understanding of theoretical concepts.
  • Exposure to modern laboratory equipment and techniques.
  • Development of critical thinking and scientific reasoning.
  • Better preparation for career opportunities in zoology.

Facilities of the Zoology Lab

The laboratory is well-equipped with modern tools and materials for practical and research purposes.

  • Invertebrate specimens for practical study and identification.
  • Prepared slides and histology slides for microscopic observations.
  • Zoogeographical maps for studying distribution of animals.
  • Microscopes and many slides for detailed microscopic analysis.
  • Other standard zoology laboratory equipment for experiments.

Zoology Lab Rules

  • Handle specimens and equipment carefully.
  • Wear lab coats and gloves when required.
  • No eating or drinking inside the lab.
  • Keep your workspace clean.
  • Follow instructor instructions carefully.
  • Report accidents or breakages immediately.
  • Do not remove lab materials without permission.
