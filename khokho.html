KHO-KHO

Kho Kho is a traditional and popular outdoor sport in India that requires speed, agility, teamwork, and quick thinking. It is played between two teams, where one team chases while the other team tries to avoid being tagged. The game is played on a rectangular field, and players sit in a row while their teammates chase the opponents by giving a “Kho” to continue the pursuit. Kho Kho helps improve physical fitness, coordination, reflexes, and strategic skills. It also teaches discipline, cooperation, and sportsmanship among players, making it an exciting and energetic game enjoyed by students across the country.
