Bachelor of Business Administration

About the Department

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) department focuses on developing students' knowledge in business management, finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, and organizational leadership.

The program prepares students for careers in corporate management, banking, entrepreneurship, marketing, and business consulting. With experienced faculty and practical learning methods, students gain strong analytical, managerial, and decision-making skills.
