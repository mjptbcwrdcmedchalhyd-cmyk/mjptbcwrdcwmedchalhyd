Department of Commerce

About the Department

The Department of Commerce is committed to academic excellence in the fields of business, trade, finance, and economics. The department aims to equip students with strong analytical abilities, ethical values, and practical knowledge required for success in the modern business world.

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs focusing on accounting, finance, marketing, management, entrepreneurship, and international trade. Our experienced faculty members combine academic expertise with industry insight to ensure students develop professional competence, leadership skills, and a global business perspective.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Commerce (Computer Applications)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (Business Analytics)

Objectives

  • To provide strong foundational knowledge in commerce and business studies.
  • To develop analytical and critical thinking skills.
  • To enhance communication and presentation abilities.
  • To promote ethical values and professional integrity.
  • To equip students with practical knowledge of accounting and finance.
  • To develop entrepreneurial and leadership qualities.
  • To familiarize students with modern business technologies.
  • To prepare students for competitive examinations and higher studies.
  • To encourage research and innovation in commerce.
  • To build global business awareness and industry readiness.
