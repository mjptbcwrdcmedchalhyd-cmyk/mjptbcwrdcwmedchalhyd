MOCK ASSEMBLY

MOCK ASSEMBLY

DEPARTMENT OF ARTS

Mock Assembly Program

Date: 06-12-2024

College: Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Telangana BC Welfare Residential Degree College for Women, Medchal & Hyderabad

REPORT

The Department of Arts organized a Mock Assembly Program to provide students with practical knowledge about the functioning of a legislative assembly and to create awareness about democratic processes. The program aimed to enhance students’ leadership skills, communication abilities, and understanding of parliamentary procedures.

  1. Students from BA (EPH) and BA (PPGEP) courses actively participated in the Mock Assembly.
  2. Students were assigned the following roles:
    1. Speaker
    2. Chief Minister
    3. Ministers
    4. Opposition Leader
    5. Members of the House
  3. Students actively participated by presenting arguments, raising questions, and defending their viewpoints.
  4. The discussions mainly focused on the following topics:
    1. Education
    2. Women Empowerment
    3. Social Development
  5. The Speaker maintained discipline and ensured the smooth conduct of the session, making the program similar to a real legislative assembly.

The Mock Assembly program proved to be highly informative, interactive, and engaging for the students. It helped them understand democratic procedures and improved their confidence in public speaking.
