Visionary Leadership – Our Principal

Smt. Dr. Radha Velchuri

Principal’s Message

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Mahatma Jyothiba Phule Telangana Backward Classes Welfare Residential Degree College for Women, Medchal & Hyderabad. Our institution is committed to empowering young women through quality education, discipline, and strong moral values. We strive to create an environment that encourages academic excellence, leadership, innovation, and social responsibility.

At our college, we believe that education is not just about earning a degree, but about building character, confidence, and competence. With the support of our dedicated faculty and staff, we ensure that every student receives the guidance and opportunities needed to achieve her goals.

We are continuously working towards providing the best academic standards, modern facilities, and holistic development programs that prepare our students to face global challenges with confidence.

I warmly invite students and parents to be a part of our institution and join us in the journey towards knowledge, growth, and success.

Smt. Dr. Radha Velchuri
Principal

Why Choose MJPTBCWRDC Medchal & Hyderabad

At Mahatma Jyothiba Phule Telangana Backward Classes Welfare Residential Degree College for Women (MJPTBCWRDC), we are committed to empowering young women through quality education, strong values, and holistic development. Our college offers a wide range of undergraduate programs supported by experienced faculty, modern teaching methods, and well-equipped laboratories. We focus not only on academic excellence but also on developing critical thinking, creativity, and leadership skills.

Our dedicated faculty members provide personalized mentorship, guiding each student to achieve her full potential. Beyond academics, we encourage participation in cultural activities, sports, seminars, and community service to ensure overall personality development.

The campus provides state-of-the-art facilities, including well-maintained classrooms, a rich library, computer labs, and comfortable residential accommodations. Women’s safety is our top priority, with round-the-clock security, CCTV surveillance, and secure hostel facilities ensuring a safe and supportive environment.

We also offer career guidance, skill development programs, and placement support to prepare students for successful futures. At MJPTBCWRDC, we foster an inclusive and motivating atmosphere where every student feels valued, confident, and ready to lead.
