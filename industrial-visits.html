Industrial Visits

Learning through Real Industry Experience

Computer Background Industrial Visit

Computer background students visited IT companies to understand software development, networking, and data management systems. The visit helped students learn about real-world applications of programming and modern technologies used in the IT industry.

Fashion Technology Industrial Visit

The Fashion Technology department visited garment manufacturing units to understand fabric production, stitching processes, and fashion design techniques. Students observed modern machinery and learned about quality control and fashion trends in the industry.

BBA Industrial Visit

BBA students visited corporate offices and industries to understand management practices, marketing strategies, and business operations. The visit helped students gain practical knowledge about leadership, teamwork, and organizational structure.
